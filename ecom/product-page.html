Since 1985, ATA Leather has been crafting premium leather goods using traditional techniques combined with modern innovation. Each piece is made by skilled artisans who take pride in their workmanship.

Materials & Care

Materials:

  • Outer Shell: 100% full-grain premium leather (1.2-1.4mm thickness)
  • Lining: 100% breathable cotton quilted lining
  • Hardware: Solid brass zippers and buttons

Care Instructions:
